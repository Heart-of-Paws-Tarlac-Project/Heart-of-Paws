ABOUT US

At Heart of Paws, we are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abandoned dogs in Tarlac City. Every animal deserves a second chance at a safe, loving home, and we strive to make that possible through compassion, care, and community support. Our mission goes beyond rescue—we also advocate for responsible pet ownership, ensuring that every pet receives the love, care, and respect they deserve. Through adoption programs, foster networks, volunteer opportunities, and donation drives, we actively work to reduce the stray population and create a kinder, more compassionate community for both animals and humans. But we can’t do this alone! Whether you choose to adopt, donate, foster, or volunteer, your support makes a life-changing difference for these animals. Be a hero to a rescued pet. Help us give them the second chance they deserve. Join us today—because every paw matters!

Cecilia Castro
